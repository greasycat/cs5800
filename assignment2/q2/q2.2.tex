\section{Question 2.2}
$$
GCD(123456789,123456788)
$$

\section{Solution}
We can use the Euclidean algorithm to find the greatest common divisor 
\begin{align*}
    123456789 &= 123456788 \times 1 + 1 \\
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
GCD(123456789, 123456788) &= GCD(123456788, 1) \\
&= GCD(1,0) \\
&= 1
\end{align*}
