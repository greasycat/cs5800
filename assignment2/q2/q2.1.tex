\section{Question 2.1}
$$
GCD(648, 124)
$$

\section{Solution}
We can use the Euclidean algorithm to find the greatest common divisor of 648 and 124.
\begin{align*}
648 &= 124 \times 5 + 28 \\
124 &= 28 \times 4 + 12 \\
28 &= 12 \times 2 + 4 \\
12 &= 4 \times 3 + 0
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
GCD(648, 124) &= GCD(124, 28) \\
&= GCD(28, 12) \\
&= GCD(12, 4) \\
&= GCD(4, 0) \\
&= 4
\end{align*}
